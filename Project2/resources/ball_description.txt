Baseball

Baseball is a sport played between two teams of nine players each who take turns batting and fielding. Players on the batting team scores runs by hitting a ball that's thrown by the pitcher with a bat swung by the batter, then running counter-clockwise around a series of four bases: first, second, third, and home plate. A run is scored when a player advances around the bases and returns to home plate.
